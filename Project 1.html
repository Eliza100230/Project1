Let me tell you about some of the abilites associated with Sombra and what they do to other players!

  1. Hack: Disables abilities on an enemy and gives teammates the ability to see hacked enemies through walls. Taking damage interupts the hacking attempt
  2. Stealth: Invisbility and able to run faster. This is a passive ability.
  3. Translocator: Throw a beacon and teleport to it. This reduces the stealth cooldown. Originally was a permanent item when placed but has since been reworked.
  4. Virus: Damages enemies over time. Hacked enemies take damage faster.
  5. EMP: Disables all enemy abilites while also inflicting damage based on current health. From experience, it's an awesome ult and you can get a full team wipe in the right conditions.
